The design features a stylized representation of a sunbeam guiding the way forward.


The term "Dream" represents the aspirations, goals, and visions that individuals and communities hold. It reflects the foundation's focus on turning these dreams into reality. "Beam" symbolizes light and guidance, indicating the role of the foundation in illuminating the path to achieving these dreams.
